Game Rules

  • Once you click on a sprite, then you get the dice rolled for you
  • If all four of your sprites win, they will be removed from the board and other players will be notified of your victory
  • In the box down below, turns are being displayed. Wait for your turn and then click on your own sprite
  • If you click on anybody else's sprite, nothing will happen
  • If you click on your own sprite and it is not your turn, a message will be displayed to you in the turn box down below to behave yourself
  • Once all 4 of your friends conenct, then the board, the dice and the turn value will be sent out to all of you
  • You can get a maximum of three consecutive sixes. After that, turn will be handed over to the next player. Your sprite, however, will not be burnt and stay there
  • In this game, there are no safe cells, i.e., your sprite gets killed no matter where it is placed on the board if some other sprite lands there
